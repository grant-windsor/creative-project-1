Grilling the Grilledest Cheese that ever was Grilled

2/1/2020 | by Grant Windsor

I just love me some good ol' grilled cheese. Nothing is better that coming home after a long day of classes, firing up the grill, and enjoying this great american meal. The pioneers themselves loved grilled cheese! I bet.

Why is it that I loved grilled cheese? Well a long time ago I met a man named Greyson Sponkiner. He introduced me the the greatest recipe for grilled cheese ever known. Even now, the Sponkiner method is known only to a few. I am choosing to pass this wisdom onto you, with faith that you will use it only for good.

You will need:

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Bacon or Ham
  • An Egg

Instructions

  1. Heat to medium heat. Spread butter as a non-stick agent

  2. Cook the egg using the Instructions here. Keep it warm.

  3. Put two slices of bread on the griddle. Sprinkle the cheese on them.

  4. Heat up your bacon, or ham. Make sure to cook it enough if it's not pre-cooked.

  5. Put it all together. Don't be timid, EAT IT!
